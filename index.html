Facts on Sun and Earth

Sun and Earth

1.Revolution

The process in which earth goes around the sun is known as revolution.
Fact: Earth takes 365 and a 1/4 days(also known as a year) to complete one revolution.

2.Seasons

Seasons are largely due to factors surrounding the Earth's tilted axis as it revolves around the sun.
A season is a period of the year that is distinguished by special climate conditions.
The four seasons—spring, summer, fall, and winter—follow one another regularly.
Fact:There are four points in earth's revolution known as solistices and equinox marking the start of the season.

3.Life

One of the many reasons Sun helps Earth is it helps to sustain life.
